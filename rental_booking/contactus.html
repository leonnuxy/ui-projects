Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking? +
You can cancel your booking directly from your account dashboard. Go to "Your Trips" and select the booking you'd like to cancel. Please note that cancellation policies vary by property.
Is my payment information secure? +
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors.
Can I modify my reservation? +
Yes, in most cases you can modify your reservation through your account dashboard. However, changes are subject to availability and the property's modification policy.
What if I have issues during my stay? +
Our 24/7 support team is always available to help. You can reach us through the contact information above, or use the emergency contact details provided in your booking confirmation.
How do I leave a review? +
After your stay, you'll receive an email invitation to leave a review. You can also access this from your trip history in your account dashboard. Your feedback helps other travelers and improves our service!
